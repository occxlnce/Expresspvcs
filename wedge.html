Our Wedge Awnings Installation Process:



At Express PVC Specialist, we take pride in our meticulous and professional wedge awnings installation process as outlined below. We aim to ensure the seamless integration of our awnings into your outdoor space while providing optimal shade and protection:

- Initial Assessment: To begin, we conduct an initial assessment of your outdoor area to determine the most suitable location for the wedge awnings. We take into account factors such as sun exposure, wind direction, and architectural features to ensure optimal placement.

- Custom Design: Once the location is determined, we custom design the wedge awnings to fit your specific requirements. Our expert team considers factors such as size, shape, and color to create awnings that complement your outdoor space and enhance its aesthetic appeal.

- Preparation: With the design finalized, we prepare the installation site by clearing any obstructions and ensuring a clean and level surface for mounting the awnings. This step is essential for ensuring a smooth and efficient installation process.

- Bracket Installation: Next, we install the brackets that will support the wedge awnings. We carefully position the brackets according to the design specifications, ensuring they are securely anchored to the wall or structure. Proper bracket installation is crucial for the stability and durability of the awnings.

- Awnings Mounting: Once the brackets are in place, we mount the wedge awnings onto the brackets using high-quality hardware. We ensure that the awnings are positioned correctly and securely fastened to provide reliable shade and protection against the elements.

- Tension Adjustment: After mounting the awnings, we adjust the tension to ensure proper operation and appearance. We carefully adjust the tension settings to achieve the desired level of tautness and smooth operation of the awnings.

- Testing and Inspection: With the installation complete, we conduct thorough testing and inspection of the awnings to ensure they meet our high standards of quality and performance. We test the operation of the awnings, check for any defects or issues, and make any necessary adjustments.

- Customer Satisfaction: Finally, we review the installation with you to ensure your satisfaction. We address any questions or concerns you may have and provide guidance on the proper care and maintenance of your wedge awnings. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to exceed your expectations with every installation.

By following this comprehensive installation process, we guarantee that your wedge awnings are installed with precision and care, providing you with reliable shade and protection for years to come.
