Soweto Johannesburg Custom Pool Cover Installation for Pretty Ndlamini
  • Client Overview: Pretty Ndlamini, based in Soweto, Johannesburg, sought a durable and stylish pool cover that would blend seamlessly with her pool area’s aesthetic. The pool area is tastefully designed with matching dark elements, creating a cohesive and elegant look.
  • Project Objective: To design, manufacture, and install a custom dark pool cover that complements the existing decor and provides excellent protection and functionality.
  • Scope of Work: The project began with an on-site consultation to understand Pretty's requirements and take precise measurements of the pool. The aim was to create a pool cover that would not only protect the pool but also enhance the overall appearance of the poolside area.
  • Challenges and Solutions: The main challenge was to ensure the pool cover matched the dark theme of the pool area, including the dark outline of the pool, the beach chair, and the umbrella. By selecting the right materials and colors, EPS was able to deliver a solution that perfectly met the client’s aesthetic and functional needs.
  • Materials Used: The pool cover was made from high-quality, UV-resistant material in a dark shade, ensuring both durability and a sleek look.
  • Project Outcome: Installed on 3 May 2024, the dark pool cover blends beautifully with the overall design of Pretty’s pool area. The cover provides robust protection and seamlessly integrates with the dark-themed decor.
Installation Date: 10 May 2024
