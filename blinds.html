Our Roller Blinds Installation Process:



At Express PVC Specialist, we take pride in our meticulous and professional roller blinds installation process as shown below in our detailed installation process to ensure your complete satisfaction:

- Accurate Measurement: First, we start by accurately measuring the width and height of your window. For an inside mount, we measure the width at the top, middle, and bottom, using the narrowest measurement to ensure a perfect fit. For an outside mount, we measure the desired overlap on each side for optimal coverage. Precise measurements are crucial for ensuring that your blinds fit perfectly and operate smoothly.

- Bracket Positioning: Next, we mark the positions for the brackets. We hold the brackets up to the window frame or wall where the blinds will be installed. For inside mounts, we position the brackets inside the window frame; for outside mounts, we position them above the window frame. Using a pencil, we mark the screw holes through the brackets, ensuring they are level and evenly spaced. This step is essential for ensuring that the blinds are mounted securely and operate without any issues.

- Hole Drilling and Bracket Attachment: Once the bracket positions are marked, we drill holes at the marked positions. If we’re drilling into drywall or plaster, we insert wall plugs to provide extra support for the screws, ensuring a secure installation. This ensures that the brackets are firmly attached and capable of supporting the weight of the blinds.

- Blind Installation: With the holes prepared, we securely attach the brackets to the window frame or wall using screws. We make sure the brackets are firmly attached and perfectly level with each other. This step is crucial for ensuring that the blinds operate smoothly and are securely mounted.

- Testing Operation: Now, it’s time to install the roller blind. We insert the pin-end of the blind into the corresponding bracket first, followed by the control-end (the end with the chain or cord) into the other bracket, twisting slightly to lock it into place. This step ensures that the blind is securely mounted and ready for use.

- Functionality Check: Once the roller blind is securely in place, we pull the chain or cord to test its operation. We ensure the blind rolls up and down smoothly without any obstructions, making adjustments as needed to ensure perfect functionality. This step is crucial for ensuring that the blinds operate smoothly and without any issues.

- Safety Installation: For added safety, especially in homes with children, we install a safety device for the chain or cord, keeping it taut and reducing the risk of accidents. This step ensures that the blinds are safe for use in homes with children.

- Final Inspection: Finally, we check that the blind is level and make any necessary adjustments. We ensure that the blind fabric hangs straight and doesn’t twist or bunch up, completing the installation to our high standards. This step ensures that the blinds are aesthetically pleasing and operate smoothly.

By following this thorough process, we guarantee that your roller blinds are installed perfectly, enhancing both the functionality and aesthetics of your space. We’re committed to providing you with the best service and ensuring your complete satisfaction with our installations.
