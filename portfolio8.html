Roodeport, Gauteng Custom Pool Cover Installation for Sipho Mkhabela
  • Client Overview: Sipho Mkhabela, residing in Roodeport, Gauteng, enjoys a spacious backyard filled with tall trees and a beautiful garden. His backyard, covered in lush green grass, provides a perfect setting for a serene and private pool area.
  • Project Objective: To install a durable and aesthetically pleasing blue PVC pool cover that complements the natural beauty of Sipho’s wide and green backyard, ensuring pool safety and protection.
  • Scope of Work: The project involved designing and installing a custom blue PVC pool cover. The installation had to ensure that the cover was not only functional but also enhanced the overall visual appeal of the backyard.
  • Challenges and Solutions: One of the challenges was to ensure the pool cover blended well with the wide green space and tall trees. Careful selection of a blue PVC cover provided a striking yet harmonious contrast with the surrounding greenery.
  • Materials Used: High-quality blue PVC material was used for the pool cover, ensuring durability, UV resistance, and aesthetic appeal.
  • Project Outcome: The installation of the blue PVC pool cover was completed successfully, adding to the charm of Sipho’s beautiful backyard. The cover not only enhances the look of the pool area but also provides essential protection and safety.
Installation Date: 19 December 2023
