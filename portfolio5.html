Brakpan Tsakane Custom Pool Cover Installation for Wisani Nwankoti
  • Client Overview: Wisani Nwankoti, a resident of Brakpan Tsakane, resides in a magnificent house with meticulously designed outdoor spaces. The property boasts tiled paving extending seamlessly to the pool area, reflecting the owner’s attention to detail and preference for elegance.
  • Project Objective: To design, manufacture, and install a custom pool cover that harmonizes with the luxurious surroundings and provides effective protection for the pool.
  • Scope of Work: The project commenced with an in-depth consultation to understand Wisani’s vision and requirements. Special consideration was given to the mansion’s grandeur and the existing design elements, including the tiled paving and white painted walls serving as fencing.
  • Challenges and Solutions: The primary challenge was to ensure the pool cover complemented the upscale ambiance of the property while providing robust functionality. EPS addressed this challenge by selecting materials and colors that would seamlessly integrate with the mansion’s aesthetic.
  • Materials Used: A dark gray pool cover was chosen to match the sophistication of the property. The cover was crafted from durable, UV-resistant material, ensuring longevity and reliable protection for the pool.
  • Project Outcome: Installed on 27 October 2023, the custom pool cover enhances the allure of Wisani’s house while effectively safeguarding the pool. Its dark gray hue complements the dark roof and white painted walls, creating a cohesive and visually appealing outdoor space.
Installation Date: 27 October 2023
