Winchester Hills Johannesburg Custom Pool Cover Installation for Rendani
  • Client Overview: Rendani, a resident of Winchester Hills, Johannesburg, required a practical and aesthetically pleasing pool cover for his home. The neighborhood of Winchester Hills is known for its serene environment and well-maintained homes, and Rendani's house reflects this standard.
  • Project Objective: To design, manufacture, and install a custom pool cover that aligns with the home’s environment and provides durable protection for the pool.
  • Scope of Work: The project involved an initial site visit to take precise measurements and discuss Rendani’s preferences. The pool cover was designed to blend seamlessly with the surroundings while ensuring optimal functionality and ease of use.
  • Challenges and Solutions: The challenge was to create a pool cover that would fit perfectly and enhance the overall appearance of the pool area. Through meticulous measurement and high-quality materials, the cover was designed and installed to meet these requirements.
  • Materials Used: The pool cover was crafted from premium, UV-resistant PVC material, ensuring durability and long-term protection from environmental elements.
  • Project Outcome: The custom pool cover, installed on 20 March 2024, provides excellent protection while complementing the aesthetic of Rendani’s home. The pool area now benefits from enhanced safety and reduced maintenance needs.
Installation Date: 20 March 2024
