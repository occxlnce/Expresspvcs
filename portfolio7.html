Eye of Africa Golf Estate Custom Pool Cover Installation for Patricia
  • Client Overview: Patricia, a resident of the prestigious Eye of Africa Golf Estate, lives in a stunning dark grey house. Her sophisticated home design extends to her outdoor pool area, creating a cohesive and elegant look.
  • Project Objective: To create a custom dark grey PVC pool cover that seamlessly blends with the house, enhancing the overall aesthetic of Patricia’s property while providing excellent protection for the pool.
  • Scope of Work: The project involved designing and installing a custom pool cover that would match the dark grey color scheme of the house and surrounding tiles. The cover needed to be durable, visually appealing, and functional.
  • Challenges and Solutions: Ensuring the pool cover matched perfectly with the dark grey aesthetic of the house and tiles was crucial. EPS carefully selected materials and colors to ensure a perfect match, achieving a seamless integration with the existing design.
  • Materials Used: A high-quality, dark grey PVC pool cover was used, providing durability and UV resistance while maintaining a sleek and modern appearance.
  • Project Outcome: The installation of the dark grey PVC pool cover was completed successfully, perfectly complementing the sophisticated aesthetic of Patricia’s home and pool area. The cover not only enhances the look but also ensures long-lasting protection for the pool.
Installation Date: May 3, 2024
